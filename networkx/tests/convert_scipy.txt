Simple Graphs
--------------

>>> G=barbell_graph(10,3)

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

>>> A=to_scipy_sparse_matrix(G)
>>> GG=from_scipy_sparse_matrix(A)

>>> sorted(G.edges())==sorted(GG.edges())

>>> G=cycle_graph(4)
